CatBot

Monster Hunter Discord Bot

Invite Vote

CatBot is dedicated to helping hunters in Monster Hunter World

CatBot is a bot dedicated to providing resources on monsters in Monster Hunter World. It helps you with different aspects of the hunt such as the monsters weaknesses in certain states, what ailments affect it and where it can be found. CatBot can also assist with plenty more! Including armor information, weapon information, and item information!
